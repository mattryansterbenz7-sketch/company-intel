Only the number takes color from the spectrum. Verdict stays primary text; reason stays secondary. Sub-dimension bars each take the color of their own score — if the composite is green but Stage is amber, that amber bar is the single signal of where to look.

9 · Open questions

↑ Skip behavior
Does "skip" push to end of queue, defer by N days, or mark as "come back later in this session"? Unclear whether it should persist across sessions. Current stance: push to end of current queue, reset on next queue-load.
↓ Save-for-later bucket
Does this create a separate "Snoozed" bucket, visible from the dashboard? Or does it just add to the existing pipeline in a new stage ("Watching")? The existing Kanban has a Watching column — suggest reusing it rather than inventing a parallel concept.
Card back-side for full JD
Should there be a flip-to-see-JD affordance (card back shows raw job description)? Or is the "What Coop found" expand enough? Flipping feels delightful but adds a mechanic; suggest holding off until we see Matt skipping cards because he wants more detail before deciding.
