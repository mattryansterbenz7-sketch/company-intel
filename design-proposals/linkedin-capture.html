LinkedIn profile capture — flow

Three-screen flow: (1) user lands on a LinkedIn profile — side panel detects the profile and shows a capture CTA with extracted fields; (2) clicking Capture opens a company-picker modal to associate with a saved company/opportunity; (3) the contact appears in the Contacts panel on that company, with a LinkedIn link.

1 · Detect + preview

sidepanel auto-detects a /in/ URL

When content.js detects a LinkedIn profile, side panel switches into "person mode" showing the extracted name + headline + current company. Capture CTA is the primary action.

with a visual "new" marker
