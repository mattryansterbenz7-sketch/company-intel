Why not three columns

Reject

Three columns turn into decoration panels. A left rail for stage nav or related companies eats horizontal space that tab content actually needs — Activity timelines, email threads, meeting transcripts all want width to breathe.

DESIGN.md rule 4 says one separator, not three. A left rail on top of the tab bar and the chat rail gives you three vertical edges competing for the eye. The tab bar already navigates; stage lives in the header; related companies belong in the Activity feed. No left rail earns its weight.

Main column gets the surface. Chat rail earns its 400px because it's the product's unique edge.
