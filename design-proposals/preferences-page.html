Save UX

Autosave on blur, inline confirmation

No Save buttons anywhere. Fields commit on blur. A small "Saved" chip appears next to the label for 1.5s, then fades. No modals, no toasts, no interruption.

Open questions

Three decisions before build

Each has a recommended stance but is worth calling out explicitly.

Where does Story Time live — Profile or Coop behavior?

Story Time is personal content (your background, the arc of your career) but its whole purpose is to shape how Coop talks about you. Put it under Profile — it answers "who you are" more than "how Coop acts."

Stance · Profile > Story Time

Four groups or five?

Considered splitting Coop behavior into "Personality" (operating principles, voice) and "Pipeline" (scoring weights, auto-rescore). Extra group adds nav weight for only 3 items split 2/1. Keep four.

Stance · Four groups

Sticky left rail on scroll?

Main column can run long for sub-sections like Resume or Story Time. A sticky rail lets users jump groups without scrolling all the way back. Cost is one more CSS position to maintain. Yes, sticky.

Stance · Sticky
