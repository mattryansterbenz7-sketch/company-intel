7 · Open questions
Three decisions before build
None are blockers for the visual model — all are behavior-level defaults.
  1. Multi-expand: should only ONE entry be expanded at a time, or multiple? (Stance: one at a time — keeps the feed scannable. But multiple would let users cross-reference email ↔ meeting.)
  2. Log-new pinned: always at top even when the feed is scrolled, or scrolls with the feed? (Stance: pinned sticky on scroll — it's the primary action on this tab.)
  3. Coop-inferred events: keep/dismiss per-event, or global off-switch only? (Stance: both. Per-event dismiss for noisy ones, global toggle in preferences for the "I don't want any of this" user.)
